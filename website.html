~Island Quest~


 Island Quest is a small cozy single player  digital game,  created using Pygame.

 The game's purpose is to explore the  basic characteristics of cozy games and  focus on emotional engagement, absence  of time or competitive pressures and the  aesthetics of comfort.

 The game includes simple exploration and  care missions, with an emphasis on a  sense of rhythm, space and emotional  response.
   ~Game Features~

 cute 2D pixelated  graphs

 third person camera

 an open world to  explore

 8 missions to  complete

 many characters to  meet

 available on  Desktop/Pc
